Hearts for Everyone is a silly webcam project made to share hearts with people on the internet. It also makes for a super cool filter (looking to hopefully implement it as some kind of camera filter in the future)!

I used google's web-based machine learning model Teachable Machine and trained it to recognize two classes: idle and hands up. Then I took the model into p5.js and replaced every 12th pixel with a heart emoji depending on how bright that pixel is to assign to the "hands up" class. And voli√†! Hearts for everyone üíñ.
