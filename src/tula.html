Tula House is a plant store in Brooklyn, NY offering a variety of botanical products and services. The new identity system positions Tula House as a playful but also knowledgeable brand.

The custom typeface, Tula Sans, is a display Sans that’s been designed referencing the nodes and internodes that make up the structural composition of plants.

An upward movement referencing the upward growth of plants is important to the Tula House brand. This can be applied in motion to both typography, illustrations, and imagery.
