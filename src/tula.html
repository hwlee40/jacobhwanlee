Tula House is a plant store in Brooklyn, NY offering a variety of botanical products and services. I designed a custom typeface along with an accompanying identity system that reflect the playful sentiment of Tula House.

The custom typeface, Tula Sans, is a display Sans that’s been designed referencing the nodes and internodes found in the structural composition of plants.

The identity system is a play off the idea of containers. Outlines of pots that would normally hold substrate and house the roots of plants in real life are repurposed to contain typography and imagery in this new identity.
