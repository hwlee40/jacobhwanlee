A chapter created as part of a collection of designer interviews conducted by RISD Juniors. I interviewed Brooklyn based creative director Ros Knopov and condensed our hour long conversation into a visually digestible yet informative set of spreads. Spreads printed on newspaper tabloids through Newspaper Club.

A significant theme during my conversation with Ros was about piecing together different interests in your creative practice and not letting one title define who you are.

Corner detail close up

The design of the spreads is a reference to this theme of parsing interests. Each section of the interview is hosted in a unique shape and is pieced together with images and pull quotes.

Beige content holders are outlined shapes of Ros’s objects
